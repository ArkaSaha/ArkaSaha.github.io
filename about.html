Who am I?

In a nutshell, I am an avid learner getting closer to becoming a computer scientist by the day. I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at CNRS@CREATE. Prior to this, I finished my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at NTU Singapore in 2022. Before that, I completed my undergraduate studies at IIT Delhi in 2018, where I was conferred the degree of BTech in Computer Science and Engineering with a Departmental Specialization in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. I hail from the steel city of Durgapur, West Bengal, India, where I completed my schooling in 2014.

My Interests

Research:

I am mainly interested in the broad area of big graph data mining and management. Currently, I am focusing on algorithms for the efficient approximation of network metrics in large complex graphs (e.g. uncertain or probabilistic graphs, social networks). However, I am open to research in other areas of big graph data as well.
